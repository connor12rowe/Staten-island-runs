Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-11-03

What we collect

  • No accounts. We don’t require logins.
  • Runs you post. When you use Post a Run, details are encoded into the shareable link itself. They are not uploaded to a server.
  • RSVPs on your device only. RSVP state is stored in your browser’s localStorage so you can see what you joined.
  • Location. If you allow location, it’s used in your browser to sort runs by distance and is not stored by us.

Third‑party services

We use OpenStreetMap tiles via Leaflet for maps and link out to Google Maps for directions. Their privacy policies apply when you use those features.

Cookies

We don’t set tracking cookies. Your browser may set standard cookies for third‑party map tiles.
